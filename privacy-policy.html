Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Arrow Entangled ("the App").

Information We Collect

The App does not require account registration. It may process limited technical data through integrated third-party SDKs for ads and billing functionality.

Third-Party Services

The App uses:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Google Play Billing for one-time in-app purchases (Remove Ads).

Local Data Storage

The App stores gameplay preferences and progress locally on your device (for example high score and purchase state flags).

How Data Is Used

  • To provide app features and gameplay functionality.
  • To serve and measure ads (if ads are enabled).
  • To process and restore purchases through Google Play.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed specifically to children under 13.
