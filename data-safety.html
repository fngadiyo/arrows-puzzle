Data Safety Summary

This page summarizes data handling for Play Console preparation. Final disclosures must be completed in Play Console Data safety form.

CategorySummary
Personal infoNot directly collected by app account system.
Device or identifiersMay be processed by AdMob/Google Play services for ads, fraud prevention, and billing.
Financial infoPurchase processing is handled by Google Play Billing (Google).
In-app data storageGameplay settings/progress may be stored locally on-device.
Data sharingLimited sharing may occur through integrated Google SDKs per Google policy.
Data deletionUsers can clear local app data via Android settings.
